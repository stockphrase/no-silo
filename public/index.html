    No Silo

    No Silo is the companion site to Alan C. Taylor’s courses at Dartmouth College. The site contains syllabi, course materials, workshops, the Open Handbook, and other related bits and pieces.

    The idea for this site came from a talk I gave to the faculty of the Institute for Writing and Rhetoric at Dartmouth College in the winter of 2020 about tearing down information silos and creating an open-source teaching culture among our faculty.
